About In the VI

Your independent guide to the US & British Virgin Islands

In the VI was created to provide practical, honest, and up-to-date information for people visiting, staying, or considering moving to the Virgin Islands.

We focus on what actually matters to travelers and expats: the best beaches, reliable vacation rentals, real estate insights, cost of living details, and local tips that make your trip smoother and more enjoyable.

Our Goal

We aim to be the most useful resource for the US Virgin Islands (St. Thomas, St. John, St. Croix) and the British Virgin Islands (Tortola, Virgin Gorda, Anegada, Jost Van Dyke, and beyond).

Why Trust Us?

This site is independently run. We personally research and test the recommendations we share. Our affiliate links help support the site at no extra cost to you, allowing us to continue creating free, high-quality content.
