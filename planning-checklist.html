Virgin Islands Vacation Planning Guide & Checklist

Everything you need to know before you go

Best Time to Visit

December to April is peak season with drier weather and fewer mosquitoes. May to November is cheaper but has a higher chance of rain and hurricanes (peak season is August–October).

What to Pack – Essential Checklist

  • Reef-safe sunscreen (required by law in the USVI)
  • Snorkel mask and fins (highly recommended for St. John and St. Croix)
  • Waterproof dry bag or phone pouch
  • Lightweight clothing and a wide-brim sun hat
  • Reusable water bottle and insulated beach bag
  • Insect repellent (especially for evenings)
  • Water shoes for rocky beaches
  • Power adapter (US outlets, but bring a surge protector for villas)

Practical Tips

Many seasoned travelers buy groceries upon arrival and cook at their villa to save money. Restaurants can be expensive. Renting a jeep is highly recommended on St. John and St. Croix for flexibility.
