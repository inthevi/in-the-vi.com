St. Croix Travel Guide

The largest and most laid-back of the US Virgin Islands

St. Croix offers a more authentic and relaxed Caribbean experience. With historic towns, excellent snorkeling, rum distilleries, and beautiful beaches on both shores, it appeals to travelers who want to slow down and explore.

Best Beaches on St. Croix

Cane Bay is famous for excellent snorkeling and diving right off the shore. Frederiksted Pier and Sandy Point offer long stretches of sand, while the East End beaches are quieter and more secluded.

Sun Protection

The sun is intense year-round. Reef-safe sunscreen is required by law to protect the coral reefs that make the islands so special.

Recommended Reef-Safe Sunscreen (2-Pack)

Practical Sun & Beach Gear

A wide-brim hat provides excellent additional protection during long beach days or exploring Christiansted and Frederiksted.

Recommended Wide Brim Sun Hat

Things to Do on St. Croix

Visit the historic districts of Christiansted and Frederiksted, tour the Cruzan and Captain Morgan rum distilleries, snorkel at Buck Island Reef National Monument, or hike in the rainforest. St. Croix rewards those who take their time.
