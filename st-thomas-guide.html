St. Thomas Travel Guide

The bustling heart of the US Virgin Islands

St. Thomas at a Glance

St. Thomas is the most visited and developed of the USVI. It offers excellent shopping in Charlotte Amalie, beautiful beaches like Magens Bay, and serves as the main hub for ferries and flights to other islands.

Beach Days & Day Trips

Whether you're heading to Magens Bay, Coki Beach, or taking a day sail, having the right gear makes a big difference. A good insulated beach bag helps keep drinks cold and food fresh — especially useful since many travelers buy groceries to prepare meals at their villa rather than eating out every time.

Sun Protection

The Caribbean sun is intense. Reef-safe sunscreen is required by law in the USVI to protect the coral reefs. A quality broad-spectrum option is essential.
