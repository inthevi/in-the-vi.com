St. John Travel Guide

The most pristine and beautiful of the US Virgin Islands

Why Visitors Love St. John

More than two-thirds of St. John is protected as Virgin Islands National Park. The island offers world-class snorkeling, quiet beaches, and excellent hiking trails. It has a more relaxed, natural feel than St. Thomas.

Top Snorkeling Spots

Waterlemon Cay is one of the best places in the Caribbean to snorkel with sea turtles. Trunk Bay has a famous underwater snorkel trail. For the best experience, a good mask and fins make a significant difference.

Beach & Boat Days

Many visitors spend full days on boats or at remote beaches. A waterproof dry bag is invaluable for keeping phones, wallets, and towels safe and dry.
