St. John Travel Guide

The most pristine and protected island in the US Virgin Islands

St. John is widely regarded as the most beautiful of the US Virgin Islands. Over two-thirds of the island is protected as Virgin Islands National Park, offering world-class snorkeling, quiet beaches, and excellent hiking with minimal development.

Top Beaches

Trunk Bay is the most famous, known for its underwater snorkel trail. Cinnamon Bay, Hawksnest Bay, and Coral Bay offer different experiences, from family-friendly to more secluded.

Snorkeling Excellence

Waterlemon Cay is one of the best spots in the Caribbean for snorkeling with sea turtles. A good mask and fins significantly improve comfort and enjoyment.

Practical Gear for the Day

Many visitors spend full days on boats or at remote beaches. A waterproof dry bag is highly recommended to keep phones, wallets, and towels safe and dry.
